## Optional Subtitle Components

AspectShift-HtoV provides optional subtitle generation and subtitle export functionality.

When subtitle-related features are used, the application may download additional third-party speech recognition components and machine learning models.

These components are not bundled with the default installer and are only downloaded when explicitly requested by the user.

### whisper.cpp

Project:

https://github.com/ggerganov/whisper.cpp

whisper.cpp provides local speech-to-text functionality used for subtitle generation.

License information is available through the official project repository.

### OpenAI Whisper

Project:

https://github.com/openai/whisper

AspectShift-HtoV may utilize Whisper-compatible speech recognition models for local subtitle generation workflows.

License information is available through the official project repository.

### GGML Whisper Models

AspectShift-HtoV may download GGML-based Whisper model files, including but not limited to English-language models, when subtitle generation functionality is enabled.

Model files remain the property of their respective authors and contributors and are governed by their applicable upstream licensing terms.
